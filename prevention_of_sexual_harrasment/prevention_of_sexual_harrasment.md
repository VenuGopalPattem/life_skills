# Workplace Sexual Harassment – Short Answers

## 1. What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

Sexual harassment happens when behavior of a sexual nature is unwelcome and makes someone uncomfortable.

This can include:

* Sexual jokes, comments, or dirty language
* Sending sexual messages, pictures, or emails
* Unwanted touching, hugging, or physical contact
* Offering promotions or threatening job loss for sexual favors

Even if the person did not mean to hurt anyone, it can still be harassment if it affects someone negatively.

---

## 2. What would you do if you face or witness such behavior?

If I face it:

* I would clearly tell the person to stop, if I feel safe to do so.
* If the behavior continues, I would report it to my supervisor or HR.
* If my supervisor is involved, I would report it to higher management.

If I witness it:

* I would support the affected person.
* I would encourage them to report it.
* I would also report it myself if required by company policy.

No one should stay silent. Reporting helps keep the workplace safe for everyone.
