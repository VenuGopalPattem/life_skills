## Grit and Growth Mindset – Pointwise Answers

### Question 1 – Grit (Summary)

1. Grit means having passion and perseverance for long-term goals.
2. Success depends more on consistent effort over time than talent alone.

### Question 2 – Growth Mindset (Summary)

1. A growth mindset is the belief that abilities and intelligence can improve with effort.
2. Learning from mistakes and challenges helps us grow stronger and smarter.

### Question 3 – Internal Locus of Control

1. Internal Locus of Control means believing that your actions determine your results.
2. It encourages taking responsibility instead of blaming external factors.
3. The key point is to focus only on what you can control to stay motivated.

### Question 4 – Key Points to Build Growth Mindset

1. Believe skills can be developed.
2. Accept the challenges.
3. Learn from mistakes and failures.
4. Seek and apply feedback.

### Question 5 – My Actions to Build Growth Mindset

1. Practice consistently every day.
2. Take on challenging tasks instead of avoiding them.
3. Treat mistakes as learning opportunities.
4. Ask for feedback and improve based on it.
